About PolyPredict

Our Mission

PolyPredict aims to democratize prediction markets by providing a user-friendly platform where anyone can participate in betting on real-world outcomes.

Our platform leverages blockchain technology to ensure transparency, security, and fairness in all betting activities.

How It Works

Users purchase project tokens, each valued at $1 USD or 1 USDT, which can be used to place bets on various prediction markets.

When a market resolves, winners receive payouts based on the odds and their bet amount, directly to their connected wallet.
